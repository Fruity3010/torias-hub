Mentor

Pair with a young leader

Mentors meet with their mentee a few times a month across a 12-week cohort. The work is light, the impact is heavy.

  • Time: ~3–4 hours / month
  • Format: Virtual or in-person
  • Need: 2+ years professional experience in tech, design, or product
