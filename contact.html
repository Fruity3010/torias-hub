Frequently Asked

Quick answers

How do I apply to a program?

Each program runs in cohorts. Visit the Programs page to see what's open and submit an expression of interest — we'll notify you when applications go live.

Do programs cost money?

Our flagship programs are free for participants. They're funded by partners, donors, and sponsors who believe in what we're building.

Can I become a mentor?

Yes — mentors are the engine of our programs. Reach out via the form above and select "Becoming a mentor" so we can route your message to the right team.

How can my organisation partner?

We work with corporates, foundations, schools, and government bodies. Visit Get Involved or send us a note and we'll set up a call.

Do you operate outside Nigeria?

Our headquarters is in Lagos but we run programs and partnerships across multiple African countries. Get in touch to discuss your region.

Are you a registered nonprofit?

Yes, Toria's Hub Youth Foundation operates as a registered nonprofit organisation. Contact us for tax and registration documentation if needed for your records.
